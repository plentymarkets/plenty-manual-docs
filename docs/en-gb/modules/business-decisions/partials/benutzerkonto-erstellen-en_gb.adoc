Creating a user account:

. Go to **Setup » Settings » User » Accounts**.
. Click on **New**. +
→ The **Create new user** window opens.
. Enter the **User name**.
. Enter the **Real name**.
. Enter the **Password**.
. Repeat the password.
// tag::email[]
. Enter the user's email address. This allows the user to receive messages from the system.
// end::email[]
. {user-access} {table}
. **Save** the settings.
