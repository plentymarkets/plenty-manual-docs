[#tse]
Technical security unit (German: TSE):: An extension for point of sale systems to ensure compliance with the legal requirements of the xref:pos:pos-legal-compliance.adoc#400[German Kassensicherungsverordnung] and the xref:pos:pos-legal-compliance.adoc#1000[Austrian Registrierkassensicherungsverordnung] regarding tamper-proof recording of transactions at the point of sale. In case of plentyPOS, the TSE is usually a built-in module included in the xref:welcome:quick-start-system-requirements.adoc#500[recommended Epson receipt printers].
